EventPlugs is a publish-subscribe platform that allow organizations, retails and professionals to share relevant contents with people.

The project is bornt from the frustration to not get up-to-date relevant content from our local organizations and retails each time we need them. some of them have a websites but they are not always up to date. For whose who are on social media platforms, once you subscribe to them, it easy to get overwhelmed by contents they share but not always relevant to all people.

So we think, it should be great to have a platform that allow people to find useful, specific, and updated information from their local organizations and retails thus saving them a lot of time and frustration. This will also help organizations, retails and customers build strong connections with their customers.

We build this tool in the hope, it will be useful to you without any guarantees.
